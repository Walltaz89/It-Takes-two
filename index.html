What the game is about

A clashing couple comteplating divorce are suddenly turned into dolls. Trapped together in different bodies cody and May must now follow the tasks given to them by a magical love guru book in order to return to their original bodies. Its a genre-bending platform adventure created purely for co-op. It is the ultimate light-hearted and touching story of the challenges in getting along

The best elements of the game

What i loved about the game.

  • graphics
  • storytelling
  • gameplay

I loved the story aspect of the game and how intertwined the story and gameplay were. In the beginning of the game I could see strain on Codys and Mays relationship and how that affected gameplay. Eventhough their world changed in a magical way they couldnt apperciate it because they were stuck together, But going futher in to the game the environments became more magical and whacky and their relationship improved. I got to see their relationship develop back into a friendship.
